Looking at the effect the COVID19 pandemic has had on the way RSV cases present, and an associated study looking at vaccine uptake

Eligibility

Inclusion Criteria:

  • Babies born after 01 September 2024 admitted to Hospital with bronchiolitis, LRTI or the first episode of an acute viral wheeze

Exclusion Criteria:

  • Any previous episode of wheeze responsive to a bronchodilator
