Performance Evaluation of the LumiraDx Point-Of-Care Device for the Detection of Influenza A/B and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV)

Inclusion Criteria:

  1. Influenza and/or RSV symptomatic adult and paediatric patients.
  2. Patients should be presenting with a fever and at least 2 of the following symptoms: congested/runny nose, sneezing, dry cough, sore throat, headaches, fatigue, weakness and/or malaise, myalgia, breathing difficulties, arthralgia, cyanosis, vomiting, diarrhoea & anorexia.
  3. Participants may be of any age, there will be no upper or lower age limit.

Exclusion Criteria:

  1. None
