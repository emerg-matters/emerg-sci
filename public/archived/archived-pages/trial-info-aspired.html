To determine whether immediate, enhanced (14-day) ambulatory ECG monitoring decreases the number of self-reported episodes of syncope at one year compared to standard care monitoring in acute unexplained syncope patients.

Inclusion Criteria:

  • 16 years old or more
  • Willing and able to provide written informed consent in English and comply with study procedures
  • A local resident
  • fewer than 5 episodes of syncope within the past month

Exclusion Criteria:

  • Obvious underlying cause
  • Obvious vasovagal syncope
  • Obvious postural hypotension
  • Diagnostic ECG
  • Patient in custody or prison

Step by Step Guide to Recruitment:

  • If you are planning to admit this patient or refer them to the Syncope Assessment Clinic (via EPIC outpatient orders) then please contact the ED Research Team. Send them a message via EPIC Chat, (edresearchteam) or phone 217907.
