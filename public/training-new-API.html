The Associate API scheme

1 If you would like to help us recruit to these studies, you might be eligible for the Associate PI scheme, which a 6-month in-work training initiative designed to provide health and care professionals with practical, hands-on experience in delivering research trials. It enables staff who do not typically work in research to work alongside local PIs, contributing to trial success while developing their own research skills.
