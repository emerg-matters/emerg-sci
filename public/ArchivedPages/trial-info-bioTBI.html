Inclusion Criteria:

  • Severe TBI GCS 13 or less
  • 16 years of age and above
  • Within the first 24 hours since the time injury

Exclusion Criteria:

  • Below 16 years of age
  • Pregnancy
  • Over 24 hours since the time of injury

Step by Step Guide to Recruitment:

If the patient is eligible:

Explain to them:
  1. Taking part in research is likely to improve the quality of their care.
  2. The concept of clinical research: There are many areas of medicine in which we do not know the perfect way to care for our patients. We undertake well-controlled, safe, trials aimed at discovering the most important ways to improve the care of patients.
  3. In reference to the specific trial they are eligible for, explain the uncertainty that exists; for example, we do not know if their condition is best managed with usual care or the alternative under investigation. There are pros and cons to both options.
  4. Reiterate that any risks are carefully controlled - research is safe, and if they change their mind they can withdraw their participation at any point.
  5. Gain verbal agreement in principle before proceeding with the steps below
