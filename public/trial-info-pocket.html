Point of care testing in emergency departments after mild traumatic brain injury


POCKET is a study of patients with minor traumatic brain injury who present to the Emergency Department. We aim to recruit patients as soon as possible after they arrive.
Clinical information and blood sampling will be collected. Blood sampling will include use of a handheld i-STAT point-of-care analyser testing for brain injury biomarkers
