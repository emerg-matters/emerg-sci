Getting involved with research

If you wish to get involved with a research study then contact the research team in your department straight away - the ways to contact your team are listed on the home page.

Not all studies need you to have completed your GCP, but most do. Ask the team first what the requirements are to get involved.

Usually, a research CV is needed for you to be authorised to enroll patients to studies. A template research CV can be downloaded from here. It's a short quick document to complete. Please fill it out and send it to your department's research team.
